Events at Alpine Haven

Nature's Pathway.

a one-of-a-kind hiking event that takes you on an adventure through some of the most breathtaking natural landscapes in the area. With multiple trails to choose from, hikers of all levels can find a path that suits them, from easy strolls through gentle hills to challenging ascents up rugged mountain terrain. Along the way, you'll experience the beauty of nature, breathe in fresh air, and connect with other outdoor enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or just starting out, the Trailblaze Trek is an event not to be missed!

Flame & Flavor Festival.

Get ready for the ultimate backyard feast at the Smoke & Sizzle Fest! This BBQ event is a food lover's dream, with mouth-watering meat and veggies sizzling on the grill and filling the air with their delicious aroma. From pulled pork and brisket to grilled corn and baked beans, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Grab a plate and fill up on all your favorites, then kick back and relax with friends and family in a fun and festive atmosphere. Don't miss out on this tasty event that's sure to satisfy your cravings for all things BBQ!
